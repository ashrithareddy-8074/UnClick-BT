How to Customize a Premium Landing Page Template Quickly

Now youve seen all the different landing page templates. Lets see how you can customize a premium template for your needs.

For this tutorial, Ill use the Proland Landing Page template from Envato Elements. This template has a minimal design. Use it to promote products.

Customize your product page with HTML so your template fits your needs. But don't worry, we've got you covered with these easy steps:

Let's get started:

1. Choose Your Landing Page Template

The Proland template comes with several different landing page templates. The first thing you need to do is to decide which one youll use.

For this tutorial, Ill be using the Pre-Order landing page. Its called index.html. So, Ill right-click on it and open it in the Text Editor.

2. Edit HTML

<div class="top-banner row m0 text-center fadeInOnLoad">
            <div class="container">
                <h2>The future of tech is here</h2>
                <p>Holistically incentivize revolutionary collaboration and idea sharing before cost effective users. Actual focused services before highly efficient human capital. </p>
                <a href="https://vimeo.com/142705963" class="btn btn-primary btn-lg video">Play <img src="images/play-btn-icon.png" alt=""> Video</a>
                <br>
                <a href="#product-choose" class="btn btn-warning btn-lg visible-sm-inline-block visible-xs-inline-block">pre-order now</a>
                <div class="row apple-watch">
                    <img src="images/apple-watch.png" alt="" class="watch_img">
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>

Once youve opened your landing page template in a text editor, youll need to edit the HTML code to include your information.

HTML code consists of HTML tags that are in angled brackets.

To edit the text, click between those angled brackets and delete the text. Then type your own. In the illustration above, I plan to change the first H2 on the page (on line 106).

Remember that you can customize your product page with HTML. Your template can be affected if you remove source code, so be careful before doing any edits.

3. Change The Styles

  <!-- Theme CSS (For Available Color Options, See Documentation ) -->
    <link href="css/themes/blue-orange.css" rel="stylesheet">

In most cases, your landing page template comes with premade color schemes. You can see which themes are available to you in the templates CSS folder under themes. To change the colors used in the template, locate the line that says:

<link href="css/themes/blue-orange.css" rel="stylesheet">.

Then, change the blue-orange.css text to a theme of your liking. I'm going to change it to teal-magent.css.

4. Set Up Your Contact Form

$to    = "test@surjithctly.in"; // <--- Your email ID here

To set up the contact form in the footer, youll need to set your email address. Open /php/contact.php file in your code editor. Then, you can change the Email ID and Subject.

5. Upload Files to Your Web Server

Once youve customized the landing page, youll need to upload it to your web server. Your hosting provider will give you the FTP details to connect with your server.

You'll need an FTP Software for this such as FileZilla. Connect with your server and open /public_html folder in your server. Then copy all HTML, CSS and JS files from your local machine to your root /public_html folder in your server.

You're done! That's how you customize your product page with the HTML of your template.

5 Quick Product Landing Page Design Tips for 2022

Creating a single product landing page is made easier with a template. If yours is from Envato Elements or ThemeForest, it already has a professional design. But if you want to take your single product landing page design to the next level, follow these tips:

1. Have Focused Design

A lot of businesses forget that a landing page is for capturing leads. That's why you need to make sure your product launch landing page centers on a single offer. As such, your design should direct visitors to where they can share their information.

2. Keep Words to a Minimum

A piece of having a focused design is reducing the number of words you use. Nothing will make visitors leave quicker than walls of text. Some ways you can keep the amount of text low is by using bullets and icons to describe key product features.

3. Feature Attention-Grabbing Visuals

Sadly, the internet is full of product launch landing pages that neglect this element. Get creative. Add photos, videos, and illustrations to your landing page.

You can even just use a bold custom font. You'd be surprised by how much of a difference it makes.

4. Play Around With White Space

As you add different pieces to your landing page, don't forget about white space. This is an important design concept. It refers to the space between elements on a page.

The space between photos and text boxes is just one product landing page example. Check out your favorite websites and how they use white space for some product landing page design inspiration.

5. Don't Use Navigation Bars

As mentioned earlier in this section, landing pages are meant to capture leads. Navigation bars on landing pages give visitors a way to avoid sharing their information. This lowers conversions. Remove this temptation by letting visitors focus on your page's content.
